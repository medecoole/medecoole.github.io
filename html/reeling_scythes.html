General Info

Reeling Scythes was originaly going to only contain scythes but it slowly branched out to contain even more weapons then originaly intended.

The mod currently adds Scythes, Double Axes and Claws.

There are also skins for the Scythes, but more on that later.

Scythes

Right-clicking will make the scythe do a sweep attack, damaging enemies in the area you are looking at. (works with fire aspect)

Negative Knockback: Instead of pushing enemies away, you reel them in after hitting them!

Skins: Sneak-Right-Clicking on a smithing table with a Netherite Scythe will change its looks! This effect is purely visual and has no effect on actual gameplay.

Double Axes

The Double Axe has two sides that are swappable by Sneak-Right-Clicking.

Both sides are able to be enchanted separately, one side (Large Side) taking Axe enchantments, while the other (Small Side) uses Sword enchantments.
